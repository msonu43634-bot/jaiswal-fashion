Replacement Policy

Replacement Policy

At Jaiswal Fashions, all sales are final and we do not offer returns on any products.

Open Box Delivery

Open Box Delivery is available in selected locations where supported by our delivery partners. Customers are advised to inspect the product at the time of delivery before accepting the order.

Replacement Eligibility

Replacement requests will only be accepted in the following cases:

  • Wrong product delivered by Jaiswal Fashions.
  • Wrong color delivered by Jaiswal Fashions.
  • Wrong size delivered due to our error.
  • Product received in damaged condition.

Damage Claim Requirements

For any damage-related replacement request:

  1. A clear unboxing video is mandatory.
  2. The video must start before opening the package.
  3. The complete unboxing process must be recorded without cuts, edits, or pauses.
  4. The shipping label and package condition must be clearly visible.
  5. The damaged product must be clearly shown in the video.

Claims submitted without a valid unboxing video may be rejected.

Product Condition Requirements

To qualify for a replacement:

  • The product must be unused and unwashed.
  • All original tags must remain attached.
  • Product stickers, labels, seals, and packaging must not be removed or damaged.
  • The product must be returned in its original condition.

Replacement Shipping Charges

Customers requesting a replacement may be required to pay applicable delivery or shipping charges as informed during the replacement approval process.

Replacement Request Time Limit

All replacement requests must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery.

Non-Eligible Cases

Replacement requests will not be accepted for:

  • Change of mind.
  • Personal preference regarding color, design, or fabric.
  • Incorrect size selected by the customer.
  • Used, washed, altered, or damaged products.
  • Missing tags, labels, stickers, or packaging.
  • Claims submitted without valid proof.

Jaiswal Fashions reserves the right to approve or reject any replacement request after verification.
