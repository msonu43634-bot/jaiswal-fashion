Our Story

Crafting comfort and building businesses since 2019.

From Madhubani to All of India

Founded in 2019, Jaiswal Fashion started with a simple vision: to provide premium quality, affordable garments directly from the factory to shopkeepers, cutting out the middlemen.

Located in the heart of Madhubani, Bihar, our manufacturing unit employs 20 skilled artisans and workers. We blend modern manufacturing techniques with a dedication to quality that ensures every garment meets our high standards.

Today, we are proud to supply to over 500 happy shopkeepers across Bihar, producing over 3,000 pieces monthly.

Meet Our Founder

Mr. SURESH JAISWAL

With a passion for quality textiles and a vision to empower local businesses, Mr. SURESH JAISWAL laid the foundation of Jaiswal Fashion in 2019. His dedication to delivering premium quality garments at unbeatable factory prices has transformed the company from a small setup in Madhubani to a leading garment manufacturer supplying across India.

"Our goal is not just to manufacture clothes, but to build trust and long-lasting relationships with every shopkeeper who partners with us. Your growth is our success."
